Cricket is a popular sport played between two teams of eleven players.

Virat Kohli is a famous Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. 
He is known for his aggressive batting style and consistency.

Sachin Tendulkar is called the "God of Cricket". 
He scored 100 international centuries in his career.

The ICC Cricket World Cup is held every four years.

MS Dhoni led India to victory in the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup.
